# TODO 11: Kessler Sage Meproteus Frame Narrative

**Context:**
Integrate Meproteus as a key narrative figure to guide players
from exploration (Phase 1) to building (Phase 2). He provides
diegetic justification for LIDAR mechanics and the "orange-world"
atmospheric conditions, a plot vehicle that can also be used for
game tutorial narratives.

## Narrative: Meproteus

- **Identity:** Former physics teacher and a telescope
  hobbyist. Witness to the cataclysmic events, the orbital
  nuclear strikes from automated Missile Defence (GDD 3.3.1
  Background Story).
  An uncle, a soliticious guide, an outsider elder, who symphatizes
  with Head's outsiderness.
  Meproteus character understands Kessler syndrome, the cataclysm,
  and the narrative about orange fog-of-war being caused by
  atmospheric fallout particles from the chaotically deorbiting
  satellite debris, as outlined in TODO 05 Orange Atmosphere
  Narrative.
- **Role:** The first "charmed" unit for new player Heads, who
  presents or gives an object to present the cause of orange
  fog-of-war storyline.
  Metheouspro narrative is expected to give the audience the
  explanation for:
  1. the use of LIDAR, to see through pervasive orange Fog-Of-War.
  2. the use of Powerstructure, and how & why it to increases LIDAR
  sight range (Earth curvature).
- **Motivation:** Despises the "Chief's lies" about the fog, their
  story to control followers, and needs to join a faction.

### Dialogue Snippets (Uncle-style)

- "The fog isn’t dust. It’s a mirror made of knives."
- "That orange? Well, the nuclear blast thermal wave created
  nitrox gas. Then the Kessler Syndrome started... nitrox gas
  reacting with the fallout isotopes. Together they scatter light,
  like orange juice."
- "But... it likes LIDAR nanometers, goes right through. Our whisper
  in darkness."
- "Put the LIDAR on a tower. The higher the sensor, the farther
  it sees. The fog hugs the ground, but the sky... the sky still
  remembers the facticity."
- "Let's just build a Powerstructure. I’ll run the LIDAR. You run
  the circus."
- "You can't see the sky, and that's a mercy. Up there, it's the
  Kessler syndrome. A crystal cathedral shattered into a billion
  shards, all at ten thousand kilometers an hour. Satellite debris
  smashing together... forever fallout.
  We didn't just lose the stars, we fenced the world in... with a
  million supersonic shards. Stuck down here, in the orange soup,
  hiding from a sky that never stops falling."
- "The Chief says the orange is caused by the enemy faction, but the
  fog has no master. It's not the factions; it's the cataclysm."

## Gameplay Integration

- **Phase Transition:** first NPC unit player charms becomes
  Meproteus, enables player to achieve Powerstructure, triggering
  gameplay transition from Phase 1 to Phase 2.
  Design, if Powestructure build needs 1 or 2 followers.
- **Visuals:** Standard entity model, as Meproteus is always
  the first successfully charmed unit.
- **Lore Object:** Add a "Notebook" (sketches, 905nm notes).
  Exploration of this object triggers narrative popups, in-game
  story on what to do.
- **Birdseye LIDAR Unlock:** Meproteus "operates" the LIDAR system
  once a LIDAR tower is constructed by the player's followers.

## Game Mechanics

- **LIDAR Scaling:** Visibility radius scale with tower height
  could be added in game mechanics.
- **Trigger:** Dialogue triggers upon the first successful
  charming of a follower, or if object card trigger is chosen,
  upon acquiring or click of the card.
- **Cataclysm Event:** Intensify "falling nuclear reactor"
  events at a time limit (GDD 4.2.3).
