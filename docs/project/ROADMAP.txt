Mutonex Project Strategic Roadmap
=================================

VISION: A "Casual Cyber-Noir" multiplayer strategy
game set on a simulated Earth. Accessible, turn-based,
and chess-like in execution.

PHASE I: ARCHITECTURAL FOUNDATION (COMPLETED)
--------------------------------------------
Focus: Establishing the world and technical stack.
- Simulated Earth logic (Simtellus).
- Unified Elixir Gameserver & WebSocket infra.
- Hardware-accelerated Lidar visualization.
- Containerized development environment.
- Content 3D model generators for Unit geometry
  and Terrain geometry from GeoTIFF.

PHASE II: TACTICAL ENGINE (CURRENT)
----------------------------------
Focus: Implementing the "Casual" core gameplay loop.
- Transitioning to Turn-Based gating (1 Turn = 17 Days).
- Develop turn based gameplay dynamics.
- Core Action System: Charming, Building, Harvest.
- Power Projection: Domain-based sight and influence.
- Source-driven, accessible game documentation.
- Terrain streaming and interactions logic.

PHASE III: SOCIAL DYNAMICS & PERSISTENCE
---------------------------------------
Focus: Multiplayer interactions and world history.
- Lobby & Voting: Player-driven ruleset selection.
- Video-phone: Remote charm and alliance forging.
- Relic System: Persistence of buildings and history.
- Multi-sector session orchestration.

PHASE IV: ATMOSPHERE & AUTHENTICATION
-------------------------------
Focus: Narrative events and strategic complexity.
- Orbital Debris Cataclysm: Mid-game entropy events.
- Advanced Units: Airpower, Activists, Fauna AI.
- Environmental feedback: Nuclear winter effects.
- User accounts and authentication.
- Production-grade hosting and swarms.
